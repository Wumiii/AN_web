How to Cancel Subscription

You may cancel your subscription at any time. After cancellation, premium benefits will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle, and no further auto-renewals will occur.

Note: Deleting the app will not automatically cancel your subscription.

For iOS Users (App Store)

  1. Open "Settings" on your device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Select "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "AI Minute Note: Smart Notebook"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
  6. Confirm cancellation
